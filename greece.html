Fira Town

Fira (Town) is the lively capital of Santorini, Greece, perched on the edge of the caldera. It offers dramatic views of the Aegean Sea, elegant white-washed houses, and winding streets filled with shops, cafes, and galleries. Fira is known for its vibrant nightlife, charming cliffside restaurants, and cultural attractions such as the Archaeological Museum and the Orthodox Metropolitan Cathedral. Visitors enjoy strolling the promenades, watching the sunset over the volcanic cliffs, and taking the cable car down to the old port ‚Äî a perfect blend of scenic beauty, history, and modern Greek hospitality.
